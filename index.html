WHO WE ARE

A community of college students and young adults (18-28) encouraging one another to love Jesus and live Jesus. We do this by gathering as a community on Thursday nights for a worship service, meeting in small groups, and hanging out at events.

MISSIONAL COMMUNITIES

Missional communities are our effort to both pour into one another and pour out into the community around us. They meet every other week in order to invest in each another's lives, discuss the Bible, and find out ways to live out our faith. We desire for these communities to be more than just a place to talk, but groups that learn to love Jesus and live Jesus
