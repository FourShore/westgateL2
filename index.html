WHO WE ARE

A community of college students and young adults (18-28) encouraging one another to love Jesus and live Jesus. We do this by gathering as a community on Thursday nights for a worship service, meeting in small groups, and hanging out at events.

LIFE GROUPS

Life groups are a place for you to get to know one another and love one another. It can be easy to feel lost in the crowd sometimes, so in life groups, we give you a chance to be known and loved. You'll dive deeper into Thursday night's teaching, go through life together, and pray for one another. Groups meet throughout the week and are always open for people to join.

For more information on any one of these small groups email smallgroups@westgatel2.org
